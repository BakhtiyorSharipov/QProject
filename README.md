Queue - is a system used to organize queues to make the service process more efficient and easier. This system allows users (customers) to take their turn online and not waste their time.
Purpose:
Queue - created to facilitate the process of taking a queue and reduce the waiting time of customers. The user can choose his turn through the application or the website and arrive at the service place at a certain time.

Features:
1. Online queue booking
2. Queue cancelation(customer: 10min, employee/admin: anytime)
3. Black list : absent>3
4. Queue history for customers
5. Review: comment and grade
6. Complaint to queue/company
7. Employee schedule management: AvailabilitySchedule
8. Statistic and reports 
9. Block/unblock customers by admin/employee
12. Queue status
11. Notification: SMS
